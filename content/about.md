# About Me

I am currently a Data Scientist at the [Culham Centre for Fusion Energy, UK Atomic Energy Authority (UKAEA)](https://www.uk-atomic-energy.org.uk/). I recently completed my PhD in Mechanical Engineering at the [Zienkiewicz Institute for Modelling, Data and AI](https://www.swansea.ac.uk/science-and-engineering/research/zienkiewicz-institute-for-modelling-data-ai/) at [Swansea University](https://www.swansea.ac.uk/).

I obtained my M.Sc. in Computational Mechanics from Swansea University in 2021 and my B.Tech. in Civil Engineering from [APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University](https://aktu.ac.in/) in 2019.

## Research Interests

- Physics-informed neural networks and neural operators
- Scientific Python software development
- Fusion energy data science and experimental analysis
- Computational mechanics and numerical methods
